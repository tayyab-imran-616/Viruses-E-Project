PRIMARY SYMPTOMS

Commonly reported indicators of the SARS-CoV-2 pathogen.

High Fever

Elevated body temperature, often the first sign of infiltration.

Dry Cough

Persistent respiratory irritation without mucus production.

Fatigue

Acute exhaustion and systemic loss of energy levels.

Dyspnea

Difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath in severe cases.
